This page was meant to inform the reader about psychiatry, which is defined as the study and treatment of mental illness, emotional disturbance, and abnormal behavior. Psychiatrists are able to prescribe medicine, unlike psychologists. One thing I've learned meanwhile researching psychiatry is that psychiatrists can use various forms of treatment to treat their patients, such as methods of psychotherapy, medication, and psychosocial interventions.
